## Content Details
✅ CHIẾN THUẬT 5: NÉ TRÁNH CÂU HỎI → RỒI TRẢ LỜI
(Avoid first, then answer)
🔹 Cách làm
Mở đầu bằng việc không trả lời thẳng


Sau đó mới chọn 1 câu trả lời


🔹 Bài mẫu (English)
To be honest, I’m a bit of a picky eater, so I don’t really have a favourite food. But if I had to choose one, I’d probably go for pizza because I really love cheese and tomato sauce in it.
picky eater: người kén ăn
picky: kén chọn
I’m very picky about..
I’m a picky learner when it comes to subject
🔹 Dịch tiếng Việt
Thật ra tôi khá kén ăn nên tôi không có món ăn yêu thích cố định. Nhưng nếu buộc phải chọn, tôi nghĩ mình sẽ chọn pizza vì tôi rất thích phô mai và sốt cà chua.
What is your favorite food?
Sample Response: To be honest, I’m an easy-going eater, so I have a lot of favorite foods. But if I had to choose one, I’d go for burger because I really like the combination of cheese and beef

an easy-going eater: người dễ ăn
combination: sự kết hợp
What’s your favorite subject?
Sample Response: To be honest, I’m a picky learner when it comes to subjects. So I don’t really have a favorite subject, but if I had to choose one, I’d go for PE because it makes me stronger and healthier

What’s your favorite type of person?
Sample Response: To be honest, I can’t choose my favorite type of person, but if I had to choose one, I'd probably go for someone with a great sense of humor because they can make me smile every day
What are the popular kinds of vacations in your country?
Sample Response: The popularity of different types of vacation in Vietnam varies significantly based on the demographics and region.

In the big cities like Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, residents like spending their vacations at the beach, such as Vung Tau, Da Nang, Ha Long. This is especially true among younger generations who are looking for delicious foods, affordable hotels, nice views, and convenient travel.

On the other hand, residents from smaller towns often like spending their vacations in the mountains, such as Sa Pa or Da Lat. These destinations offer good food, beautiful views, and various forms of entertainment. Moreover, these trips provide a unique cultural experience where relationships between local people and travelers are often built over time.
Sample Response: The popularity of different types of vacation in Vietnam varies significantly based on the demographics and region
In big cities like Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh, residents like going to beaches or mountains on vacation, especially among younger generations who are looking for relaxation and enjoyment on their vacation
On the other hand, in smaller towns and rural areas, people like going to big cities because they want to experience the convenience in the big city and play more games in the mall. For them, these places provide a sense of excitement and a chance to enjoy modern entertainment that is not available in their quiet hometown

Sample Response: The popularity of different types of vacation in Vietnam varies significantly based on the demographics and region.

In smaller towns, the shopping malls in big cities are a good choice for a vacation because younger generations need something that is new and modern, such as cinemas and game centers. Many people can try some new food from many countries, and this provides them with a completely new experience that they cannot find in their rural hometowns. These modern facilities offer a great environment for family bonding and entertainment

On the other hand, in big cities like Hanoi, people prefer to choose some natural places like beaches. There, they can swim, play volleyball and take photos. For them, the beach provide memories and fun journey
