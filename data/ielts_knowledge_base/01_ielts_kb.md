Part 1: Social media

Does your family use social media platforms? 
Do you think Vietnamese users spend lots of time on social media?
Why do people still use social media?
Do you use credit cards?
Do you think you will stop using cash in the future?
Part 2: Describe a useful object at your home that you cannot live without.

You should say:

What it is
What you can do with it
How often you use it
And explain why you cannot live without it

Part 3:

Do you think we need to understand computers on a deeper level?
What is the most impactful piece of technology in our time?
How do computers affect our everyday life?
What modern technological devices are most common in your country?
What are the advantages of modern technology?
